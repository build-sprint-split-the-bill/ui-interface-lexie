Splitting the Bill

Figuring out how to split the bill has never been easier. Split is here to help you keep track of who owes you money for that last trip to that new Tapas bar or French tasting menu restaraunt. A one-stop app that allows you to charge your friends an even split of the bill. We have partnered with many local restaurants in major metropolises around the US. We have launched in Los Angeles, Portland, Seattle, and New York so far.
